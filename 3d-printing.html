Additive manufacturing

3D Printing Gallery

3D printing has become one of my strongest hands-on technical hobbies. This gallery documents prints I have completed, tuned, repaired, or adapted, with notes about materials, settings, troubleshooting, and model source.

Some models are my own work; others are prints of publicly available models where the value was in setup, tuning, material choice, manual filament swaps, or problem solving. This work connects naturally to software development: iterative debugging, configuration management, documentation, hardware constraints, and practical user-centered design.
